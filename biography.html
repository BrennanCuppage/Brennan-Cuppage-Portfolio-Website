About me

Well, if you've made it this far you either like what you saw on my homepage, you came here to get a sense about me first, or you're hopelessly lost. Whatever the case may be, welcome! I hope you enjoy your stay. It's a pleasure to meet you.

My name is Brennan Patrick Tyler Cuppage, and I'm an aspiring game developer, animator, and 3D modeller. This website, as time moves forward, should be a consistently growing list of my projects and accomplishments, to document where I've been, what I can do, and how much I've grown. Ever since I was young, I've loved diving into new and creative worlds, be that through video games or books or movies. As I grew up, I knew I wanted to start creating worlds of my own. Game development and 3D art allow me to do that, helping to bring life and shape to what I have in my brain and get it out into bytes and into the world.
